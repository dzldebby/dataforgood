Volunteer Process

General steps for volunteers

  • Screening

    Making sure the fit is right

    Conduct interviews and screening of volunteers to ensure that motivations and skillsets are aligned

  • Matching

    Aligning skills and interests with the needs of the non-profits

    Data For Good will match the interested volunteers with the non-profits to maximise the usage of skills

  • First Meeting

    Introductory Session

    Faciliated by Data For Good, the volunteer and the non-profit will meet to formalise the arrangement and services required. The volunteer will also be required to sign a non-disclosure agreement.

  • Exploration

    Relationship building between volunteer and non-profit

    At Data For Good, we understand the need to build context into the relationship. The volunteer and non-profit can continue to work on projects.
